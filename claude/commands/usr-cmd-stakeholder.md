Reframe the following technical content for a non-technical stakeholder audience (CEO, PM, or client level).

Rules:

- Lead with the business or user impact, not the technical mechanism
- Replace jargon with plain language without oversimplifying to the point of being inaccurate
- Keep the core tradeoffs intact - do not hide risk or complexity, just express it in terms of outcomes
- Structure: what the situation is, why it matters, what the options are (if applicable), what is recommended and why
- Tone: direct, professional, confident - not apologetic or overly hedged
- Length: as short as the content allows. No padding.

Do not invent context. If something in the input is ambiguous, note it rather than guessing.
